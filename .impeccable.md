# Design Context

## Users
- **Target Audience**: Administrative staff, supervisors, and sales managers of Fuvex (associates of BCP).
- **Context**: Used in office or field environments to manage credit sales, user hierarchies, and regional assignments.
- **Job to be Done**: Monitor sales production, manage team structures, and ensure operational efficiency in credit placements.

## Brand Personality
- **Voice**: Professional, reliable, and corporate.
- **Tone**: Sophisticated, efficient, and institutional.
- **Personality**: High-end Administrative, Premium SaaS, BCP-Branded.
- **Emotional Goals**: Confidence, authority, and clarity.

## Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual Tone**: Clean, high-contrast, professional admin dashboard.
- **References**: Stripe, Vercel Dashboard, BCP Corporate Identity.
- **Theme**: Primarily Light Mode (Institutional), but structured for high legibility.
- **Key Colors**: BCP Blue (#002A8D), BCP Orange (#FF7800).

## Design Principles
1. **Institutional Authority**: Every element should feel official and aligned with BCP's corporate standards.
2. **Functional Sophistication**: Clean layouts that prioritize data density without feeling cluttered.
3. **Hierarchical Clarity**: Use spacing and typography to clearly distinguish between operational data and strategic KPIs.
4. **Responsive Integrity**: The dashboard must remain fully functional across all viewports, especially for field supervisors.
